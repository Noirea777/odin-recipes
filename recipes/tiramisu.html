Tiramisu Recipe

Back to Home

A classic Italian tiramisu recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup mascarpone cheese
  • 1/4 cup strong coffee, cooled
  • 2 tablespoons liqueur (optional)
  • 200g ladyfingers (savoiardi)
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder for dusting

Steps:

  1. Mix egg yolks and sugar in a large bowl until pale and thick.
  2. Add mascarpone cheese and mix until smooth.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk coffee and liqueur together.
  4. Dip ladyfingers quickly in the coffee mixture and layer them in a dish.
  5. Spoon half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers.
  6. Dust with cocoa powder and repeat layers.
  7. Chill for at least 4 hours before serving.
