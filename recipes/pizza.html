Pizza Recipe

Back to Home

A delicious homemade pizza recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.)

Steps:

  1. Mix flour, water, yeast, sugar, and salt in a large bowl.
  2. Knead the dough until smooth and elastic.
  3. Let the dough rise for 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  5. Roll out the dough on a floured surface.
  6. Spread tomato sauce over the dough.
  7. Top with mozzarella cheese and your favorite toppings.
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden brown.
