Brownie Recipe

Back to Home

A delicious homemade brownie recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Mix flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together melted butter, sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  3. Gradually combine the wet and dry ingredients until just mixed.
  4. Pour the batter into a greased 9x9 inch pan.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
